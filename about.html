About Me

Bio

I'm a creative developer and design enthusiast with a deep passion for crafting accessible and beautiful web experiences. My journey into web development started from a curiosity about how things work on the internet, and it quickly evolved into a love for creating digital experiences that people enjoy using.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading business books, exploring new places, playing football with friends, or enjoying good food. I believe in continuous learning and always staying curious about new technologies and design trends.

My Goals in This Program

Through this program, I aim to strengthen my foundation in web development and push my skills to the next level. My specific goals include:

  • Mastering modern JavaScript frameworks and understanding when to use each one effectively
  • Building fully accessible web applications that everyone can use, regardless of ability
  • Learning best practices for writing clean, maintainable, and scalable code
  • Understanding testing methodologies and implementing comprehensive test coverage
  • Collaborating with other developers and learning from their approaches and perspectives

Areas Where I Need to Build Confidence

I believe honesty about my weaknesses is the first step toward growth. Here are areas where I'm still building my confidence:

  • Advanced state management in complex applications - knowing when to use context, Redux, or other solutions
  • Backend development and database design - I'm more comfortable with frontend but want to be full-stack
  • Performance optimization techniques and understanding when they're actually necessary
  • DevOps practices, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment strategies beyond basic hosting
  • Design systems and creating cohesive visual languages across large applications
